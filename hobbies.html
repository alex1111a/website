HOBBIES

Below I list my hobbies and a breif synopsis about each one.

Building PC's

In the past I have built a total of roughly 3 PC's not counting helping out my friends. I learned how to do this pretty much on my own with the help of Linus Tech Tips.I currently have an AMD Ryzen 7 3700X for a CPU, RTX 2070 super as my GPU, a b550 motherboard, 32gb of ram (3200MHz), and roughly 2.25 TB of storage.

Mechanical Keyboards

Over the course of the Covid-19 pandemic(circa 2020) I fell deep into the rabbit hole of custom mechanical keyboards and how to make them. I have made a total of 2 currently and plan to make mre once I get the funds. I am currently using a keyboard named the GamaKay LK67 that has foam in the back of it to keep it more muted, everglide aquakings(Lubed) as the switches, and I also have keycaps closley resembling the GMK botanical set.

Gaming

I have enjoyed gaming pretty much as far back as I can remember, whether it was my first time playing Minecraft or my first turn at the wheel in Mario Kart Wii, I have pretty much always been immersed in a game. Although over the years it is hard to not see a few that stand out from the rest. My personal all-tim favorite game is the Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim; from exploring the depths of the deepest caves to climbing a mointain to fight a dragon, there is seemingly nothining beyond the limits of the player in this mastepeice od a game. now when I say masterpeice, I don't say that wholeheartedly as it is obvious that because of its time, the game engine had it's limits, but it is one of my favorites bcause of the bountiful landscape and the masterful storytelling
